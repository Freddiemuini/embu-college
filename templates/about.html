About Embu College

Transforming Lives Through Quality Technical Education Since 1995

Our Story

Established in 1995, Embu College has been a beacon of technical and professional education in Eastern Kenya. For over 28 years, we've been committed to nurturing skilled professionals who excel in their chosen fields.

We offer quality diploma, certificate, and artisan programmes in ICT, Business, Engineering, Hospitality, Automotive, and Beauty courses, serving students from across Kenya and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide quality technical education that empowers students with practical skills, ethical values, and global competencies needed in today's dynamic job market.

Our Vision

To be the premier center of excellence in technical training in Africa, recognized for innovation, quality, and student success.
